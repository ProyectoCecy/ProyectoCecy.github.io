    AFFIRMATIVE


    Estructura:


    Subject +

    Auxilary +

    Verb in the Past +

    Complement



    Example in Past: 1.- Fernando has driven for 5 years 2.- Antonio has missed his ex-girlfriend

    NEGATIVE


    Estructura:


    Subject +

    Auxilary +

    Not +

    Verb in the Past +

    Complement



    Example: 1.- Dilan and Kevin have not kissed anyone
    2.- Centeno and Dilan have not gone on the superman

    Estructura:
